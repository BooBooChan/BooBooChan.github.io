Bridget's Favorite Animal Type

The hummingbird is one of my favorite animals since I learned about them in third grade. Hummingbirds are small, colorful birds, and are the only species of bird able to hover in midair with their wings (as opposed to birds that accidently hover by stupidly flying against the wind current). They fly incredibly fast, such that spotting them feels like spotting a shooting star.


Their diet is also pretty unique compared to birds. They don't eat seeds or dead carcuses. Instead, they prefer to drink flower nectar, or at least some sugar-laden liquid. They are able to feed off of flowers by reaching inside with their narrow, almost syringe-like beaks.
